Our Story

Patience is the
art of knowing
when to set sail.

Pazienza, Italian for "patience," is more than a name. It is a philosophy. The belief that the most profound experiences in life reveal themselves to those who approach the world with unhurried intention.

Built for the Pacific, she moves through waters that few vessels are capable of. From the coral-fringed atolls of French Polynesia to the wild volcanic coastlines of Papua New Guinea, Pazienza takes you beyond the map, to places most will only ever read about.

On Board

Every comfort considered

Master Suite

A full-beam master cabin with his-and-hers ensuite and panoramic ocean views from the pillow.

Private Chef

A dedicated culinary team serving bespoke menus, from dawn coffee on the foredeck to lantern-lit dinners at anchor.

Spa & Wellness

An onboard spa with treatment room, infrared sauna and gym with free weights designed for those who spend their days in the sea.

Water Sports

Sea-bob, paddleboards, kayaks, kitesurfs, dive equipment, a jet ski, an inflatable platform, and a custom tender. All launched from the aft beach club.

Connectivity

Starlink internet throughout the vessel, for those who want to stay connected, and fast enough to let you forget you're at sea.

Cinema Lounge

A dedicated screening room with 4K projection, surround sound, and deep-seating, perfect for evenings at anchor.

24-hour Crew

A professional crew of captain, engineers, chefs, deckhands, and stewardesses always on standby whenever you need around the clock.

Sun Decks

Three open decks, including a Jacuzzi sundeck and shaded alfresco dining, designed for life lived outdoors.
